建业旅游

河南建业新生活旅游服务有限公司，创办于2016年5月18日，注册资金一亿元人民币为建业集团投资设立的子公司。建业旅游秉承建业集团“追求卓越，坚忍图成”的企业精神与“根植中原，造福百姓”的核心价值观，依托建业品牌优势、客户资源及服务网络整合内外部优质资源，为客户提供个性化、差异化、定制化的旅游服务。

Company uphold the company group "the pursuit of excellence, perseverance figure into" spirit of enterprise and "grounding zhongyuan, serving the people" core values, relying on the company brand advantage, network integration of internal and external quality resources, customer resources and services to provide personalized, differentiated, customized travel service.
